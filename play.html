running

I run. I run quite a bit, when I'm healthy, and relatively fast in comparison to many people who run nowadays. I am a member of the New York Road Runners, USATF, and the Dead Runner's Society.

I'll put up a race record one of these days, but I've won seven (mostly small) races in 2002 and taken second in four more. Yes, I've run marathons: started five, finished three. (Marathons aren't easy, no matter what Team in Training tries to sell you.) I did Maine '98, Marine Corps '98, Columbus '99 , Boston 2000 and Baystate 2002. You decide which I finished! One day I'll put a database of race reports online so you can read where I've run and what I thought of it.

I was the online editor for Runner's World for five years, from 1996 to 2001. I started writing about running while I was there, and since then I've been published in RW, Running Times, New England Runner, and several websites including fast-women.com, mensracing.com, and nycmarathon.org. You can find a collection of links to those articles on the clips page.
